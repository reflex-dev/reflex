Building a Reflex component package no longer corrupts `pyi_hashes.json`. The build hooks now only generate the `.pyi` stubs bundled in the wheel and leave the hash registry alone — previously a single-package build (including the one triggered by `uv sync`) replaced the whole registry with just that package's entries, wiping every other hash. `scripts/make_pyi.py` gained a `--force` flag, merges into the registry instead of pruning it when given explicit targets, and treats an unreachable last-run commit (after a branch switch or rebase) as a full regeneration. CI now fails when a `pyi_hashes.json` entry no longer has a matching `.py` source.
